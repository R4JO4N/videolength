About This Website

This website allows you to upload multiple video files (MP4, AVI, MOV, etc.) from your local folder and automatically calculates the total duration of all the selected videos. The total duration is displayed in a readable format (hours, minutes, and seconds). It's designed to help you easily get the cumulative length of your videos.

How to Use

  • Select one or more video files by clicking the "Browse" option.
  • Click "Calculate" to calculate the total duration.
  • View the total duration of all videos in hours, minutes, and seconds.
